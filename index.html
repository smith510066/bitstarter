Today travel is not easy or cheap...

  • You need lots of time to find the cheapeast available fare,
  • You need time and money to buy luggage
  • Even more money to get the proper visa for the country you are visiting
  • Need to plan for time off from work
  • You need the patience of dealing with airport security
  • Plan all the logistics of getting to and from the airport in your home city and destination city

So why go thru all this hassle when there's iTrotter? With iTrotter you can visit they most beautiful adored cities in the world not only from the comfort of your own home, but for a fraction of the cost!

The solution...

DoubleRobotics iPad teleconferencing robot is a new technology which allows you to be literally be in 2 places at once -- even worldwide. Combined with the idea of iTrotter (and your pledge and support) we plan to first open a travel office in London - a city that is known for its Modern elegance and historical charm. Our office will staff 4-5 travel guides who will be with our iTrotter visitors who will be viewing and site seeing London (and many other cities) to come. Our travel guides will be with our iTrotter visitor every step along the way to show them famous museasums, landmarks and sites!

FAQ

Besides London, do you have any plans to start an iTrotter office anywhere else?
Yes, of course! To get the ball rolling we wanted to begin in London, but we anticipate opening offices in Paris, Hong Kong and Sydney!
What are the drawbacks to iTrotter?
Good question! Mainly you can't physically experience your trip fully as if you were actually there. But we feel the benefit and cost saving of not having to pack up and travel half way around the world out weights this!
What is our pledge to support iTrotter going towards?
Another good question! First, setting up an office in London with a staff of 4-5 travel guides. Second, it will go towards marketing and PR mainly in the US for our service. As a backer you'll be given a 1 hour tour of London as a reward for your support and belief in our idea/service!
